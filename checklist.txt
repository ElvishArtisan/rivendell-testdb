                           Rivendell Test Checklist

Test 001 [ ] - Start rdairplay(1) and load log 'test-001' ['Audio Play-out 
               Test']. Verify that you see three carts:

                  090011 - 1 kHz, 2 channel PCM16
                  090025 - 1 kHz, 2 channel PCM24
                  090027 - 1 kHz, 2 channel MPEG Layer II 256 kbit/sec

               Press 'Start' for the first cart and verify that all three
               carts play a 1 kHz tone at reference level for 10 seconds.
